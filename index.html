About Me

I am a Undergraduate Computer Science & Statistics Student at UC Berkeley graduating in May 2024.

My Interest lies in Distributed Systems and Cloud Computing. I'm looking for new ways to run services on the cloud and build architectures that help with large workloads. I use insights from different domains (from Microservices to Software Defined Networking) to improve the stability and throughput of large systems.

I'm currently working on SkyPlane: Inter-Cloud bulk data transfer interface, advised by Professor Ion Stoica and Joseph E. Gonzalez.

I'm also working on JIPCAD: A Joint-Interactive-Procedural CAD Tool used for mathmatical 3D modeling, Advised by Professor Carlo H Sequin.
